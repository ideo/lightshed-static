WAVES OF GRACE
OVERCOMING THE HORROR OF LIBERIA’S EBOLA EPIDEMIC
After surviving a bout with the deadly disease that stole the lives of her family, Decontee, a Liberian native, uses her immunity to help children orphaned by Ebola. This VR experience follows the Liberian tragedy from illness to recovery, mourning to perseverance.
